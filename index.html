Research Focus

Research Areas

Integrating transportation planning, data science, and environmental analysis to inform sustainable mobility policy.

🔋
EV Infrastructure Planning
Optimizing highway charging station placement using Location-Allocation modeling, entropy weighting (TOPSIS), and traffic assignment. Examining infrastructure demand under 10/30/50% EV penetration scenarios.
Location-Allocation TOPSIS Traffic Assignment GIS
📡
Dynamic Wireless Charging
Assessing modal shift and environmental impact of DWCS using stated-preference surveys and discrete choice models on the Seoul road network. Quantified: +14% EV market share, 140.82B KRW air pollution reduction (10 yr).
SP Survey Discrete Choice DWCS Network Simulation
📊
Transportation Policy Analysis
Evaluating EV subsidy and incentive policies through behavioral modeling. Analyzed EV competitiveness under varying operating costs, estimating 4.8–11.0B KRW/year in societal air pollution reduction benefits.
Binary Logit Policy Evaluation Cost-Benefit EV Subsidy
